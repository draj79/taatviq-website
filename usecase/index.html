Where TaatvIQ Delivers Impact

In today’s dynamic business landscape, organizations across industries are inundated with data — yet struggle to convert it into real-time, actionable intelligence. TaatvIQ bridges this gap by offering a fully-managed, vertical-agnostic analytics platform that transforms raw operational data into powerful insights. Built on a modern streaming architecture and embedded AI capabilities, TaatvIQ is designed to seamlessly plug into your existing systems, adapt to unique workflows, and enable smarter, faster decisions — no matter your industry. Whether you're managing energy grids, optimizing factory floor performance, tracking retail behavior, or analyzing shipping routes, TaatvIQ empowers teams to stay ahead with timely, tailored, and trusted analytics.

Energy

Track and predict energy usage across grids, plants, or facilities in real-time. TaatvIQ identifies inefficiencies, forecasts demand spikes, and enables better load balancing using streaming sensor data and AI-driven consumption models. It also supports green energy initiatives by tracking carbon emissions, analyzing renewable energy contributions, and delivering actionable insights to optimize sustainability and compliance.

Manufacturing

Monitor production throughput, quality anomalies, and inventory flow. TaatvIQ integrates with shop-floor data and ERP systems to deliver insights that improve OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness) and reduce downtime through predictive analytics. It also supports sustainability and ESG initiatives by enabling energy usage analysis, emissions tracking, and waste reduction strategies in manufacturing operations.

Retail

Detect footfall patterns, real-time stockouts, and customer behavior across locations. From personalized promotions to optimized staffing, TaatvIQ empowers retail decisions with live dashboards and intelligent triggers. It also enhances operational efficiency by integrating point-of-sale, inventory, and supply chain data to deliver real-time insights that improve conversion, reduce stockouts, and optimize in-store experiences.

Maritime

Gain operational visibility into fleet performance, fuel consumption, port delays, and cargo movement. TaatvIQ integrates AIS, weather, and port data to enhance safety, optimize routing, and reduce turnaround time. It also enables proactive maintenance, voyage planning, and real-time exception handling by unifying data across vessels, ports, and logistics systems.
